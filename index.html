Avalanche rescue dogs are asked to do things that would scare most dogs: fly in helicopters, ride on snowmobiles, walk through mountain snow at night with the sun's reflection off the moon as the main source of light, and get on their handler's shoulders so they can be skied down the mountain.

Classic movies in live action comedies would often display small barrels of brandy around their necks. Avalanche victims supposedly drank the brandy to stay warm while awaiting rescue, although this is medically unsound, but who cares? It's badass.
